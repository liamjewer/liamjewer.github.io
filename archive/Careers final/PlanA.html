Daily Duties

  • Design mechatronics systems using computer aided design
  • Program control systems for mechatronic systems
  • Write engineering reports about their designs
  • Test their products to make sure they efficiently complete the desired task

Working Conditions

Mechatronics engineers work at high-tech companies developing new technology, they can work at a desk, on a factory floor. Mechatronics engineers work a standard 5-9 job (40 hours per week), and can travel to conferences to present new designs.
