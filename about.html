About Me

I’m Liam Jewer, and I am an Engineering Systems and Computing student at the University of Guelph with a minor in entrepreneurship. I hope to build a career in engineering and start my own business one day. I have a passion for software development and mechanical design. I believe systems engineering is a perfect fit for me because it mixes all types of engineering and looks at systems as a whole. I was a member of First Robotics Competition Team 3683, Team DAVE, for 5 years and participated in all aspects of developing the robot. In my final years on the team, I was a lead programmer. I learned a lot and passed my knowledge on to younger students.
