About Me

I’m Liam Jewer, Founder and Primary Creative Consultant at Jewer Designs. I am a self-taught software development enthusiast from Waterloo Ontario. I make stunning, responsive, interactive websites using my knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP -- check out my previous works on my websites page. My software development skills extend beyond websites... I also use Java and occasionally Python and Visual Basic. I am an active member of the software sub-team on FRC (First Robotics Competition) Team 3683 (Team DAVE), where I use Java to program a robot to complete a different challenge annually. I will be pursuing a career in Engineering. A career in Engineering will help fulfil my love for software development and mechanical design. I will continue to make websites because it is a passion of mine and It will always remind me of where I started programming. Check out my site, and contact me if you have any questions.
